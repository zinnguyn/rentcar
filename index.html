How Its Works

Rent with 3 easy steps

Choose a location

Step one
Choose a location near you or the location where you want to rent a car. To find the most suitable location for you, please refer to our operating area.

Pick-Up Date

Step two
Select the date you want to rent the car and the date you want to return it. We will deliver the car to you at 9:00 am on the rental date and you must return it by 5:00 pm on the return date. If you want to change the time slots, please contact us through the hotline. When changing the fixed time slots, we will charge an additional fee of 100$/1 hour.

Book a car

The final step
Just click send and we will contact you to confirm the car rental schedule. Once completed, you just need to wait for us to bring the car to you. You can also choose self-driving or hire our driver when we contact you to confirm the car rental schedule.
